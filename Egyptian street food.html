Egyptian meat shawarma

  • Ingredients
  • 500g beef (cut into cubes), 3 tablespoons oil, juice of 1 small lemon, 1 medium onion (halved, half for marination and half with vegetables), 3 crushed garlic cloves, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon black pepper, 1 teaspoon meat spices, 1 teaspoon paprika, 1/4 teaspoon ground cardamom, half a bunch of parsley, 1 large tomato, 1 hot pepper (optional), oil for frying. For the tahini sauce: 3 tablespoons tahini, 1 teaspoon vinegar, juice of half a small lemon, a pinch of salt, cumin, coriander, chili powder, and water. Instructions: 1. Mix the beef with oil, lemon juice, half the onion, crushed garlic, salt, black pepper, meat spices, paprika, and ground cardamom. 2. Let the meat marinate for at least 1 hour. 3. While marinating, prepare the vegetables: chop the tomato, parsley, and the remaining half onion. 4. Heat oil in a wide pan, then add the marinated beef and fry until cooked. 5. Add the vegetables (tomato, parsley, onion, and hot pepper if using) and stir them with the meat. 6. Prepare the tahini sauce: mix tahini with vinegar, lemon juice, salt, cumin, coriander, chili powder, and gradually add water until you reach a liquid consistency. 7. Serve the Kekla with tahini sauce on the side.

Crepes

  • Ingredients
  • 1 cup flour, 1 cup milk, 3 tablespoons oil, 2 eggs, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon sugar, 1/4 teaspoon white pepper. Instructions: 1. In a large bowl, whisk the eggs well. 2. Add the milk and oil, and mix well. 3. Add the flour, salt, sugar, and white pepper, and mix until you get a smooth batter. 4. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. 5. Pour a small amount of the batter into the pan and wait until bubbles form on the surface. 6. Flip the pancake and cook the other side until golden brown. 7. Repeat the process until the batter is finished. 8. Serve the pancakes hot with honey or your favorite syrup.
